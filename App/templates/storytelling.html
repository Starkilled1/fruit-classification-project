Storytelling

This Fruit Identification Model is designed to help users identify various fruits using machine learning.

The model has been trained using a comprehensive dataset of fruit images, enabling it to recognize and classify different types of fruits accurately.

Our goal is to make fruit identification accessible and easy for everyone, whether for educational purposes, food industry applications, or personal interest.

Explore the different functionalities of our model through the provided API endpoints, and learn more about how machine learning can be applied to real-world problems.
