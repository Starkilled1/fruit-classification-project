Model 1 Analysis

The graph depicts the training and validation accuracy of Model 1 over 10 epochs. The training accuracy shows a steady increase, indicating that the model is learning and fitting the training data progressively better. In contrast, the validation accuracy exhibits a slight initial rise followed by fluctuations, and it remains substantially lower than the training accuracy. This pattern suggests that while the model is becoming more accurate on the training data, it is not generalizing well to the validation data, indicating possible overfitting.
